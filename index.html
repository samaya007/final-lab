Electro

Electro Wizard is a legendary card in Clash Royale

The Electro Wizard has moderate hitpoints and damage output, but its primary strength lies in its ability to stun and reset enemy troops and buildings. Whenever it attacks an enemy troop or building, it will stun them for a brief moment, resetting their attack or special ability.

Witch

The Witch is a epic card in Clash Royale,

The Witch is a ranged troop with moderate hitpoints and damage, but her main strength is her ability to summon skeletons. Whenever she attacks an enemy troop or building, she will spawn a group of skeletons that will target and attack nearby enemies.

Giant commonly knows as electro giant is a rare card in Clash royal.

Wizard

The Wizard is a common card in Clash Royale

The Wizard has moderate hitpoints and high damage output, and it attacks by casting fireballs at enemy troops and buildings. Its splash damage makes it effective against groups of enemy troops, as it can damage and take out multiple units at once.

Dragon

The Dragon is a epic card in Clash Royale

Commonly known as baby dragon is a flying troop that deals splash damage to enemy troops and buildings. The Baby Dragon has moderate hitpoints and damage output, and its main strength lies in its ability to fly over ground troops and attack from the air. Its splash damage makes it effective against groups of enemy troops, and it can also target and take out enemy buildings.
